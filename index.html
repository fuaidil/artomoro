About Our Company

Artomoro Prima Utama is a company that focuses on providing the best solutions in construction, supply, and general trading. With high experience and dedication, we are committed to providing quality, timely and efficient services to every client. We always prioritize professionalism, innovation, and building good relationships with partners and customers. Supported by a team of experienced experts and a network of trusted suppliers, we are ready to meet the needs of construction projects, procurement, and trade in a comprehensive and sustainable manner.

Services

What We Do?

General Contractor

We provide general contracting services covering the development of small to large-scale construction projects, from planning to execution. With a team of experienced and committed professionals, we ensure every project is completed with high quality, on time, and within budget. We focus on building infrastructure such as paving blocks and other facilities with an innovative and sustainable approach.

General Supplier

As a general supplier, we provide a wide range of materials and equipment for the industrial and construction sectors. We understand the importance of quality and delivery accuracy in every project, so we only provide products from suppliers that have been tested and guaranteed quality. Artomoro Prima Utama is ready to become a trusted partner in meeting the material needs for various projects, from small to large scale.

General Trading

Our company is also engaged in general trading, providing a wide range of quality products from various industry sectors. With an extensive network of partners and suppliers, we are able to meet market needs with reliable products. Our commitment is to provide competitive prices, quality products, and fast and responsive service to ensure customer satisfaction.
